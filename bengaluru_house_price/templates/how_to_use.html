How to Use the Price Predictor

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select the location of the property
  2. Enter the total square feet area
  3. Specify the number of bathrooms
  4. Enter the number of BHK (Bedroom, Hall, Kitchen)
  5. Choose your preferred prediction model:
    • Linear Regression: Simple and interpretable
    • Random Forest: High accuracy, good for complex patterns
    • Decision Tree: Easy to understand, handles non-linear relationships
    • Support Vector Regression: Good for handling outliers
  6. Click "Predict Price" to get the estimated price
